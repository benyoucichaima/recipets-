Dish Name: Dough Borek


Dough ingredients:
  • White flour
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Water /milk
  • Yeast

Filling ingredients:
  • Minced meat
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Herbs
  • Spices: salt, black pepper.

reparation Method: In a bowl, place two cups of warm water with a little salt and yeast, and half a cup of oil. Mix the liquids well, then gradually add the flour until we get a soft, elastic dough. Let it rest for half an hour while we prepare the filling. In a pan, add the finely sliced onions with salt and black pepper and cook until softened, then add the ground meat and cook thoroughly. Let it cool a little. Return to the dough, which has risen for 30 minutes. Divide the dough into small portions, then roll them out until they are somewhat transparent. Place the filling on top and roll them up like a cigar. Repeat this process with the remaining dough. Heat a generous amount of oil in a frying pan and fry the dough, turning it repeatedly until it is cooked. Enjoy your meal!
